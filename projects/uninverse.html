TTS System of K-pop Fandom Platform, “UNIVERSE” (live service)
Mar 2019 - Apr 2022 (@ NCSOFT)

I contributed to the research and development of a multi-speaker TTS system capable of generating the voices of approximately 100 artists within a single TTS system. These TTS systems are utilized in the following two services included in UNIVERSE, which is a K-pop fan community platform serviced by NCSOFT.

1. Fan Networking Service (FNS)

The K-pop artists create posts with photos and brief comments, similar to Instagram, and our TTS system reads these comments aloud in the respective artists' voices.

2. Private Call

UNIVERSE offeres simulated phone calls to fans featuring their favorite artists' voices. Our TTS system is employed to generate these lifelike artist phone calls.
